The Bittercube Bazaar

The Bittercube Bazaar is an eclectic milwaukee cocktail destination offering unique spirits and liqueurs, in tandem with vintage glassware, books, craft bar tools plus an assortment of Bittercube Bitters.

Place your  order online and pick it up for contactless service. Or safely browse the shop in person as only one group is allowed in at a time and surfaces are sanitized between groups. To check out the offerings, visit the bazaar shop.

We are also leading online cocktail classes and the occasional online facility tour, to check out upcoming events, visit our events portal. At this time, the Bittercube Bar is closed until further notice.
