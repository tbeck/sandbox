---
layout: syrup_recipe
name: Cucumber Syrup
description:
ingredients:
  - name: Cucumber
    quantity: 1600 g
  - name: Granulated sugar
    quantity: 1600 g
  - name: Pectic enzyme
    quantity: 4 g
  - name: Water
    quantity: 50 g
instructions_html: >-
  <p>Blend sugar, cucumber flesh, in a large cambro. Add 4g pectin enzyme to
  cambro. Let sit overnight. Add 50g of water, mix to amalgamate all sugar,
  strain solids.</p>
---
