Bittercube Bar

A unique cocktail destination in Milwaukee’s Uptown Crossing neighborhood. Our passion for making drinks come true is showcased in every aspect of the Bittercube Bar.

The menu reflects that passion with a broad spectrum of libations - showcasing cocktails we’ve featured in bars across the country through our consulting efforts, as well as featuring seasonal Tailor Made highballs and On the Fly Elixirs, plus a Dock18 Cocktail Lab section featuring products currently in R&D.
