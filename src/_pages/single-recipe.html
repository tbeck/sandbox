Name of Cocktail

Ingredients
  • 1.5 oz. Partida Blanco Tequila
  • .5 oz. Del Maguey Mezcal (Vida, Santa Domingo or Chichicapa)
  • .25 oz. Agave Nectar
  • 11 drops Bittercube Orange Bitters
  • 5 drops Bittercube Cherry Bark Vanilla Bitters
Notes

Garnish: Orange Peel

Stir ingredients, strain into rocks glass with ice and garnish.
